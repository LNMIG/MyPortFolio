About Me

I am a passionate Web Full Stack Developer, looking for sharing my knowledge and experience with an innovative company. I am experienced in the use of JavaScript, Nodejs, React & Redux, PostgreSQL, Express, Python, Django, MySQL, HTML and CSS.

In addition, I am a Structural Engineer with 20+ years of professional experience in both American and Argentinian AEC industry. I am experienced in structural design, and familiar with American codes, such as ASCE7, AISC, ACI, CBC, NDS, ADM, and IBC.

My experience as a professional Structural Engineer during the past 20 years allows me to bring to any company considerable understanding, knowledge and know-how about project management, teamwork, work under pressure, divide large projects into smaller pieces and conquer the expected result and more skills that will enrich any actual working team. Also, I feel proud of my ability to foresee the final result or possible problems since the very start of a project.

To sum up, I consider myself a problem solver and client oriented professional.
